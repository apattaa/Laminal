// 003 — first game

Veilborn

2D Action · Exploration · Surreal Dark Fantasy

A lone wanderer awakens at the edge of a crumbling world held together by forgotten rituals. The land shifts between states — day becomes myth, architecture breathes, and nothing stays the same twice. Explore a vast, handcrafted world that rewards curiosity over combat.

Procedural LoreWorld history that evolves from your choices and discoveries.
Dynamic BiomesEnvironments that shift based on time, secrets found, and rituals performed.
Metroidvania StructureUnlock abilities to peel back hidden layers of the world.
Handcrafted WorldEvery corner designed with intention — no procedural filler.
